Known issues

Playback of playlist videos fails
A problem that seems to occur with builds using DVDPlayer. It works fine on xbox builds with mplayer

General information

This plugin is written for The mediacenter, also known as XBMC and can be found at xbmc.org.

It's main purpose is to scrape media content from NRK and feed it to XBMC as virtual folders.
